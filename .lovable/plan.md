

# NextGen HQ Tech — Enterprise Website Plan

## Overview
A modern, dark navy SaaS-style website for NextGen HQ Tech, a B2B lead generation and call center company. Single-page scrolling layout with smooth animations and glass-effect header.

## Pages & Sections (Single Page)

### Header (Fixed, Glass Effect)
- Logo + "NextGen HQ Tech" + tagline on left
- Hamburger menu (left-aligned) opening a slide-out nav with: Home, Services, Process, Clients, About, Contact
- Transparent/glass blur effect that becomes solid on scroll

### Hero Section
- Bold headline: "High-Performance Lead Generation & Professional Call Center Solutions"
- Supporting subheadline about outbound campaigns and expert call handling
- Two CTA buttons: "Book Free Consultation" and "View Services"
- Subtle animated background pattern or gradient

### Services Section (6 Cards)
- Cold Calling, Lead Generation, Outbound Sales, Customer Support, Email Marketing, Website Development
- Each card: icon, title, short description, hover scale/glow animation
- Grid layout (3×2 on desktop, 1 column on mobile)

### Process Section (4 Steps)
- Strategy → Setup → Execution → Optimization
- Horizontal timeline on desktop, vertical on mobile
- Numbered steps with icons and brief descriptions

### Stats Section
- 250+ Clients | 98% Satisfaction | 24/7 Support | Global Coverage
- Animated counter effect on scroll

### Client Testimonials
- 5 testimonial cards with client name, company, country flag emoji, and review text
- Carousel or grid layout

### About Section
- Brief company overview and mission for B2B markets (USA, Canada, UK, Australia)

### Contact Section
- Form: Name, Email, Subject, Message, Submit button
- Contact info sidebar with email (nextgenhqtech@gmail.com) and phone

### Footer
- Company description, quick links, social media (Facebook linked to facebook.com/nextgenhqtech)
- Contact email, phone, copyright notice

## Design
- **Theme**: Dark navy (#0a1628 range) with blue/cyan accents
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif, large headings, professional spacing
- **Animations**: Fade-in on scroll, hover effects on cards, smooth section transitions
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first with hamburger menu and stacked layouts

## SEO
- Meta title, description, and keywords in index.html
- Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy
